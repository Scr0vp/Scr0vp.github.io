Key Features

Maze Navigation

The player navigates through a fixed 2D maze with walls and corridors, using simple controls to move up, down, left, or right.

Enemy

Eenemies are scattered throughout the maze. The player must shoot and eliminate these enemies to clear a path or reach the maze's exit.

API Integrations

The maze contains health packs and ammo pickups that the player can collect to replenish their health and ammunition.
